# Notify Studio

Build, test, template, organise, audit, and diagnose rich Home Assistant Companion notifications.

## v0.1.16 highlights

- Manage membership from each custom category or area with **Select entities** and source-card checkboxes.
- Use individual member controls or an overall category/area control to enable or disable saved automations.
- Every group/member control displays its local custom category or area tag.
- Notification source cards expand naturally without internal scrollbars.
